VectorDBZ

A compact, evidence-first AI information aggregation platform.

VectorDBZ collects code, model, paper, Reddit, news, and job-market signals, then turns them into small cited reports and answerable evidence instead of another noisy feed.

v1.0 clean baseline v2.0 source-aware harness runtime ClickHouse + Qdrant + FastAPI

What changed

  • Source taxonomy instead of generic news rows.
  • Evidence contracts before LLM narration.
  • Ask-with-citations responses that can refuse weak evidence.

How it runs

  • Collectors write to ClickHouse.
  • Embeddings land in Qdrant.
  • Rerank and reports run as resumable phases.

Operational rule

  • Deploy clean commits or tags.
  • Keep secrets in server-local env files.
  • Scan before every public push.
